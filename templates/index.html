Inventory Manager

About

This is an inventory manager that allows the user to add products,
from a predefined list of products to an inventory. The user can also,
remove products from the inventory or check which products are currently,
in the inventory.

Usage

To add a product to the inventory go to the products page and,
under "Add product to inventory" type in the name of the product,
you want to add, then enter the quantity and click the "Add" button.

To remove a product from the inventory go the inventory page and,
under "Remove a product from the inventory" type in the name of the,
product you want to remove, then enter the quantity and click the,
"Remove" button. If you want to remove all products from the inventory,
use the "Clear" button.
